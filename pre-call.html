BEFORE THE CALL

Have these ready when we speak.

The more prepared you are, the more specific and useful the audit will be. You do not need to have all the answers. Just have access to the information.

Your current monthly revenue and what it was 3 months ago. The trend matters as much as the number.
Your approximate close rate. How many of the sales conversations you have end in a signed contract. A rough number is fine.
Your average deal value. What a typical new client pays per month or per project.
Where leads are currently coming from. Inbound, referrals, outbound, paid. Even roughly.
Access to your CRM or pipeline, or be ready to describe what your current follow-up process looks like step by step.
The single biggest revenue problem you want to solve, in one sentence. If you have not thought about this, think about it now.
